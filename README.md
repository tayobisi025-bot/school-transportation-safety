# School Transportation Safety

Blockchain solution designed to enhance student transportation safety through comprehensive route monitoring, driver certification tracking, and emergency communication systems. Specifically built for school district transportation departments managing complex bus operations.

## System Overview

This contract manages the complete transportation ecosystem - from driver certification and vehicle maintenance schedules to real-time trip logging and emergency response protocols. Each component integrates seamlessly to provide district-wide visibility and safety compliance.

- **Driver Certification Management**: Tracks license validity, background check dates, and certification renewals with automated expiry monitoring
- **Vehicle Fleet Operations**: Maintains inspection schedules, mileage tracking, fuel efficiency monitoring, and maintenance alerts  
- **Route Optimization**: Links certified drivers to appropriate vehicles while monitoring student capacity and safety ratings
- **Emergency Response**: Severity-based alert system allowing drivers and administrators to report incidents with timestamp tracking

## Practical Applications

The system prevents assignment of expired certifications to active routes and maintains detailed trip logs for compliance audits. Emergency alerts can be filed by authorized personnel with automatic escalation based on severity levels. Student registration includes special needs flagging and emergency contact management for comprehensive safety coverage.
